Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and network setups.

How long does a typical installation take?

Most residential installations are completed in 2-4 hours, depending on the size of your home and complexity of the setup. Business installations typically take 1-2 days to ensure everything is properly configured and tested.

Do you provide equipment or should I purchase it myself?

We can provide all necessary equipment at competitive prices, or we can install equipment you've already purchased. We're happy to provide recommendations based on your specific needs if you prefer to buy your own.

What areas do you service?

We currently service the greater metropolitan area and surrounding suburbs within a 50-mile radius. For locations outside this area, please contact us for availability.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance plans?

Yes, we offer monthly and annual maintenance plans that include regular check-ups, security updates, and priority support. These plans are customized based on your network size and requirements.

What if I experience issues after installation?

We provide free support for the first 30 days after installation. After that, you can opt for one of our maintenance plans for ongoing support and troubleshooting.
